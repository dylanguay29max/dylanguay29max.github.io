How To Fence Foil

Fencing is a sport that many people think of as sword fighting. However, there are many rules to the sport that people don't know. Right now, I will explain to you how to fence with a blade called a Foil, since this is the blade that I fence with.

Gear

To fence with Foil, you will need this gear:

  1. Foil mask (helmet)
  2. Foil lame (electrical piece that covers the torso)
  3. Fencing jacket (goes under lame for extra protection)
  4. Fencing pants (optional)
  5. Under-arm protector (optional)
  6. Chest protector (optional)
  7. Mask cord and body cord (Important cords that connect all electrical components)
  8. Foil blade
  9. Fencing glove

Rules For Fencing Foil

Now that you know the gear to fence Foil, here are the main rules.

  1. Right of way (To gain right of way, you must be advancing or beat the opponent's blade. Whoever has the priority will get the point if they hit the opponent)
  2. You must hit with the tip of your blade on either the lame, or the bib of the mask.
